\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class representing a tridiagonal matrix, i.\+e. an matrix of the form. 

\[A = \begin{pmatrix}
 a & c & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
 b & a & c & \cdots & 0 \\
 0 & b & a & \ddots & \vdots \\
 \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & c \\
 0 & \cdots & 0 & b & a
\end{pmatrix},
\] where b=c=-1/h^2 and a=2/h^2.
